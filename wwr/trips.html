Our Featured Rafting Adventures

Thunder Rapids Challenge

Conquer the legendary Class IV Thunder Rapids, perfect for thrill-seekers. Expect big waves, fast currents, and non-stop excitement. Includes safety gear, a certified guide, and a riverside lunch.

Calm River Family Float

A gentle Class I-II ride designed for families and first-timers. Enjoy stunning scenery, wildlife spotting, and plenty of splashy fun without intense rapids.

Canyon Run Expedition

A two-day Class III-IV adventure through breathtaking canyons. Camp under the stars and enjoy a full outdoor dining experience while navigating thrilling rapids by day.
